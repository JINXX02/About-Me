Experience

U.S. NAVY

From October 2020 to October 2023, I served in the U.S. Navy as a PACT Seaman, gaining hands-on experience in ship maintenance, operations, and team coordination. I was responsible for preserving the ship’s exterior and interior spaces, assisting with small boat operations, and operating equipment such as JLG lifts. I stood critical watches both underway and in port, contributed to damage control and emergency response efforts, and played a key role in transferring supplies at sea. My time in the Navy strengthened my ability to work under pressure, solve problems, and collaborate effectively with a team.
